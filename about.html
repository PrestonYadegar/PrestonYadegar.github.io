About Me

I'm the Founder-CEO of Shareholder Vote Exchange, the world's first exchange for shareholder voting rights. We believe that innovative markets and technologies have the potential to improve corporate finance and governance by opening up the competition for corporate control.

I had previously started Sicilian Investment Group, a macro hedge fund to manage investments for my family and friends.

I had interned at Quantopian and briefly at the SEC.

I studied Philosophy and Computer Science at Boston University.
I grew up in, and currently live in, the San Francisco Bay Area.
